Loan & Application Explanation

Confident & Reassuring Great, [Caller’s Name] — again, my name is [Agent Name], and I’ll be guiding you through this process today.
So just to give you a quick overview — the money you receive from the loan will wrap up all your unsecured debt — into one loan — and one repayment. We work with the largest lending network in the country and rates start in the single digits. (Pause for a response.)
Absolute Certainty So the first step is to gather some information for your loan application and if everything lines up, you could be funded as soon as today. Sound good? (Pause for a response.)
Awesome. So as part of the application, we’ll do a soft credit check — that just helps us verify your balances and make sure we’re not missing any accounts that could be added to your consolidation loan. Does that make sense? (Pause for a response.)

Perfect — let’s go ahead and get started.

Application Requirements

What loan amount are you looking to get approved for today?

Okay, and are you currently active in the military? Do you currently hold a security clearance?

Employment & Income

Okay got it, and what is your current employment status?
How often are you paid — weekly, bi-weekly, semi-monthly, or monthly?
And what is your gross annual income?
Do you own your home or rent?

Education

Perfect. And just for the application, what’s the highest level of education you’ve completed?
If client needs more clarification:
This is just one of those standard lender questions — what’s the highest level of school you’ve finished?
Example: Associate degree, Bachelors, High School, Masters… still in school
If client asks why we need that:
Good question. Some lenders use it as part of their risk model, kind of like employment length — it doesn’t approve or deny you by itself.

And I just need your Social? (Repeat back 9 digits.)
Permission Close Got it. And do you give us permission to run your soft credit check to finalize your application?

They’ll just need to ask a few additional questions to make sure we’re getting you the best option possible and finalize your eligibility. You won’t need to start over — they’ll already have everything you’ve shared so far.
Before I do that, I just need your permission to receive text messages related to your application — like verification codes or important updates. Is that okay?
Perfect. I’m sending that code now — once you get it, can you read it back to me?
Great, thanks! Let me get you over to your Advisor now.

Your financial advisor will be calling you back within 2 hours or less to go over your loan application from a “949 area code” — They will be texting you prior to calling you as well.
