Direct Mail Script Overview

This script covers the Direct Mail channel for Horizon Financial Services. Prospects receive a physical loan offer letter in the mail and either fill out an online form, call in directly, or are reached via outbound calls through Answer Connect.

The call flow covers three entry points (Outbound Form Fill, Outbound Answer Connect, and Inbound), then converges into a shared verification, application, and credit pull process.

1. Outbound Call — Form Filled

Use this opening when the prospect filled out an online form in response to the mail offer.
AGENT: Hi, is this [First Name]?
Pause for a response.
AGENT: Hey [First Name], this is [Agent Name] with Horizon Financial Services — I have your online submission for the loan offer letter you received from us.
AGENT: I just wanted to know if now's a good time to go over everything with you?
Pause for a response.
AGENT: Perfect. On the first page of that letter, you will see an 8–11 digit "Invitation Code" (pin code) — could you read that to me please?
CRM NOTE: Enter the invitation code into the search field in Motion CRM.
AGENT: One moment while I pull up your information.
Proceed below the "red line" to the Verification & Info Gathering section.

2. Outbound Call — Answer Connect

Use this opening when the prospect left a voicemail/message via Answer Connect.
AGENT: Hi, is this [First Name]?
Pause for a response.
AGENT: Hey [First Name], this is [Agent Name] with Horizon Financial Services — It looks like you called in and left a message to receive more information on the loan offer letter you received from us.
AGENT: I just wanted to know if now's a good time to go over everything with you?
Pause for a response.
AGENT: Perfect. On the first page of that letter, you will see an 8–11 digit "Invitation Code" — could you read that to me please?
CRM NOTE: Enter the invitation code into the search field in Motion CRM.
AGENT: One moment while I pull up your information.
Proceed below to the Verification & Info Gathering section.

3. Inbound Call

Use this opening when the prospect calls in directly.
AGENT: Thank you for calling Horizon Financial Services, this is [Agent Name]. Were you calling about the letter we sent you in the mail?
AGENT: Perfect. On the first page of that letter, you will see an 8–11 digit "Invitation Code" — could you read that to me please?
CRM NOTE: Enter the invitation code into the search field in Motion CRM.
AGENT: One moment while I pull up your information.

Fallback: No Invitation Code

If the prospect does not have their invitation code, use this flow instead.
AGENT: Okay, that's fine. I'll just need to get the correct spelling of your first and last name to pull up your information in our system.
CRM NOTE: Enter the name in the search field in Motion CRM, then continue to Verification below.

4. Verification & Info Gathering

All call types converge here after pulling up the prospect's information.

Confirm Identity

AGENT: Okay, I got your information pulled up and just to confirm — am I speaking with [Caller's First and Last Name]?

Verify Address

AGENT: Okay, great, and I have your current address at [street number and name, city, state, and zip]?
CRM NOTE: If the address is different, update the new address in Motion CRM.

Address Duration

AGENT: And have you been at that address for at least 2 years or more?
IMPORTANT: If YES → you will only need the last 4 digits of their SSN later. If NO → you will need the full Social Security Number.

Confirm Phone

AGENT: And is the phone number you're calling in on [XXX-XXX-XXXX] the best number to reach you at?
If not: "What number would you like us to contact you at?"

Get Email

AGENT: Perfect, and what's the best email address for you?
IMPORTANT: Repeat each letter and number for the email and confirm.

5. Loan & Application Explanation

AGENT: Great, [Caller's Name] — again, my name is [Agent Name], and I'll be guiding you through this process today.
AGENT: So just to give you a quick overview — the money you receive from the loan will wrap up all your unsecured debt into one loan and one repayment. We work with the largest lending network in the country and rates start in the single digits.
Pause for a response.
AGENT: So the first step is to gather some information for your loan application and if everything lines up, you could be funded as soon as today. Sound good?
Pause for a response.
AGENT: Awesome. So as part of the application, we'll do a soft credit check — that just helps us verify your balances and make sure we're not missing any accounts that could be added to your consolidation loan. Does that make sense?
Pause for a response.
AGENT: Perfect — let's go ahead and get started.

6. Application Questions

AGENT: What loan amount are you looking to get approved for today?
AGENT: Okay got it, and what is your current employment status?
AGENT: How often are you paid — weekly, bi-weekly, semi-monthly, or monthly?
AGENT: And what is your gross annual income?
AGENT: Do you own your home or rent?
AGENT: Perfect. And just for the application, what's the highest level of education you've completed?

If Client Needs Clarification on Education

AGENT: This is just one of those standard lender questions — what's the highest level of school you've finished?
Examples: Associate degree, Bachelor's, High School, Master's, still in school

If Client Asks Why Education Is Needed

AGENT: Good question. Some lenders use it as part of their risk model, kind of like employment length — it doesn't approve or deny you by itself.

7. Soft Credit Pull & File Submission

AGENT: Okay thank you for that and we're almost done here. The last step is a soft pull on your credit. It won't show up as a hard inquiry or impact your score — it just gives us what we need to verify your accounts.

Collect Date of Birth

AGENT: What's your date of birth?
Repeat back DOB to confirm.

Collect SSN

If at address for 2+ years:
AGENT: And I just need the last four digits of your Social Security Number.
Repeat back the 4 digits.
If at address for less than 2 years:
AGENT: And I just need your Social Security Number.
Repeat back the 9 digits.

Get Permission

AGENT: Got it. And do you give us permission to run your soft credit check to finalize your application?
CRM NOTE: Enter the SSN and click "I Agree, Run Soft Credit Check." IMPORTANT: If there are 4 extra digits on the zip code, erase them and save. You can only pull credit on a 5-digit zip code.

Hold & Prepare File

AGENT: Great. One moment while I prepare your file.
Place the call on hold for approximately 20 seconds.

8. Motion CRM Steps

Complete these steps in Motion CRM while the client is on hold:
  • Change status from "New Lead" to "Apped"
  • Select "Debt Consolidation" for Loan Purpose
  • Select any Credit Rating when creating the app (correct it after you pull credit and find the actual score)
  • Pull Credit in the Debts tab
  • Select and review any qualified accounts to include
  • Assign the lead to yourself
